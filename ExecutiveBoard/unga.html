UNGA DISEC


The United Nations General Assembly First Committee (also known as the Disarmament and International Security Committee or DISEC) is one of the six main committees of The United Nations General Assembly. Since this committee falls under the General Assembly, all member nations have equal representation. DISEC considers matters of disarmament and international security that lie within the scope of the Charter, threats to peace that affect the international community and seeks out solutions to the challenges in the international security regime. DISEC works in close cooperation with the United Nations Disarmament Commission and the Geneva-based Conference on Disarmament. It is the only Main Committee of the General Assembly entitled to verbatim records coverage.

Agenda


1. Addressing the proliferation of Cluster Munitions and their adherence to International Humanitarian Law (IHL)
2. Preventing the acquisition of Chemical, Biological, Radioactive, and Nuclear (CBRN) materials by non-state actors.
