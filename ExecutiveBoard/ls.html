Lok Sabha


A house divided against itself cannot stand" Lok Sabha, the house of the people, is the very embodiment of the spirit of democracy with representatives of the people, from the people and elected by the people. The representatives here, are as varied as the the languages they speak, the religions they profess and the cultures they practice. With such diversity however, come many many divisions, intensified by the inherent politics influencing debate. It is up to the House to put aside these differences, and to make decisions that will grant our motherland her rightful place in the annals of history.
